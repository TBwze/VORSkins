History

VOrskins is an Indonesian multinational retailer of personal care and beauty products. Featuring nearly 340 brands, along with its own private label, VOrskins Collection, VOrskins offers beauty products including cosmetics, skincare, body, fragrance, nail color, beauty tools, body lotions and haircare.

Vision

To supply Indonesia with the finest quality skincare and cosmetics.

Mission

Keep maintaining the quality of our products so it can always satisfy our customers.
